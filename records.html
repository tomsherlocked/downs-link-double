Records



Here's the latest records for the double. For a record to count it must be completed as per the Self Supported UK guidelines. In essence, no support, start times, no prizes. Attempts can be made at any time, just email the details to the downslinkdouble@gmail.com email, including a GPX/Strava/Komoot ride, plus some photo proof of your start/mid/endpoints. There's an element of goodwill involved too, it's up to you to self-police your attempts in the ITT spirit.
